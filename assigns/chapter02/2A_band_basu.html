Favorite 3 CD releases

  1. Aar Jani Na

    2. Review: It was the first CD by a Bengali band that popularized the use of guitar in Bengali music. It appeled to the young generatation the the most and lead to the birth of band groups in Bengali music tradition. It was also one of the most commertially successful CD in late 90s.

  2. Gadha

    3. Review: Inspired by the heavy sale of the first album, Chandrabindoo the second album Gadha the next year. The song "khelche Sachin" celebrating cricket sport icon Sachin Tendulkar bacame very popular. "Roga Bolo Na" - composed with much satire was also highly successful.

  3. Twaker Jatna Nin

    4. Review: The third album came out in 1999 with 11 songs with a slightly different tone and melody. Althugh they kept their satire in their songs, they wrote about lower middle class and pointed out the problems in our educational system. This album was also cordially welcomed by the mass.
